Changes made
- src/codegen/regalloc/color.rs: aligned hard-register color numbering with OCaml select/color semantics. Caller-saved hardregs receive low colors and callee-saved hardregs receive high colors; GP color map now matches OCaml simulation: 0->R9, 1->R8, 2->SI, 3->DI, 4->DX, 5->CX, 6->AX, 7->BX, 8->R12, 9->R13, 10->R14, 11->R15.

Colored pseudos are rewritten to hard registers; uncolored pseudos remain as spill markers for replace_pseudos to place on the stack. Coalescing and iterative spill rewriting remain out of scope.
- src/codegen/regalloc/allocate.rs: preserves used GP callee-saved registers with push/pop and stack-alignment padding when needed.

- src/codegen/regalloc/operands.rs: Pop is now modeled as writing its destination register for liveness instead of failing, so existing assembly streams with Pop can be analyzed.
- src/compiler.rs + src/pipeline.rs: `--no-coalescing` path invokes regalloc before replace_pseudos; default/coalescing-enabled path remains unchanged to preserve existing chapter 18/19 gates because coalescing is not implemented in this task.
- .omo/evidence/task-58-coloring-probe.rs: replaced stub-only probe with a durable production-source probe that compiles production `types.rs`, `operands.rs`, `graph.rs`, `simplify.rs`, and `color.rs` together, then asserts OCaml color mapping, callee-saved reporting, reserved registers, hardreg conflicts, and spill marker behavior.
